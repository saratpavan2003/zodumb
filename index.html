What is Zodumb?

Zodumb is a free, AI-powered funny horoscope generator. Enter your birthday and get a hilariously unhinged cosmic reading based on your zodiac sign — part roast, part fortune cookie, zero accuracy. No sign-up, no account, just instant chaos you can share.

Cosmic Beef — zodiac compatibility, roasted

Switch to Cosmic Beef to check the zodiac compatibility between two people. Drop in two birthdays, choose how you know each other — dating, crushing, friends, siblings, roommates, coworkers, exes, or sworn enemies — and Zodumb returns an absurd compatibility percentage, a red-flag and green-flag count, and a ridiculous cosmic verdict. It is the pettiest compatibility calculator on the internet.

How it works

  1. Pick Solo Reading for one birthday, or Cosmic Beef for two.
  2. Enter the date(s) of birth and, for compatibility, the relationship type.
  3. Get an instant AI horoscope or compatibility score.
  4. Download a share card and post your fate.

Frequently asked questions

Is Zodumb real astrology?

No. Zodumb is pure comedy. The horoscopes, compatibility percentages, and flags are all invented by AI for entertainment. Accuracy is not guaranteed — chaos is.

How does the compatibility score work?

Zodumb reads both zodiac signs and the relationship type you picked, then makes up a funny compatibility percentage and verdict. It is a punchline, not a measurement.

Is Zodumb free? Do I need an account?

It is completely free and needs no sign-up. Enter a birthday and get your reading instantly.

Can I share my horoscope?

Yes. Every reading can be turned into a downloadable share card for Instagram, WhatsApp, or your group chat.
